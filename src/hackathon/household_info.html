Recommendations

  • Conserve Energy: Use energy-efficient LED or CFL light bulbs, Use natural lighting during the day to minimise artificial lighting needs.
  • Reduce Water Usage: Fix any leaks in faucets, pipes, or toilets promptly, Install water-saving fixtures like low-flow toilets and aerated faucets.
  • Practice Recycling and Composting: Create a designated recycling area in your home for paper, plastics, glass, and metal, Start composting organic waste like food scraps and yard debris for use in gardening.
  • Use Eco-Friendly Products: Use cloth towels instead of paper towels whenever possible, Look for energy-efficient appliances with high Energy Star ratings.
  • Promote Sustainable Transportation: Walk, bike, carpool, or use public transportation whenever feasible, Opt for telecommuting or remote work options to reduce daily commuting.
